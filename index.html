About

I conduct research in the field of public policy and administration, as part of my PhD in Social and Political Science at Bocconi University, in Milan.

Before starting my PhD I worked for two years in a government advisory company in the MENA region, expanding my professional skills and policy knowledge.

I hold a MSc in Public Policy and Administration from the London School of Economics (2022) and a BSc in Economics and Management from Bocconi University (2021).

Currently I am a member of the Graduate Student Advisory Board of the International Public Management Journal.

Research and Publications

Currently, my research is focused on two broad streams: technology in government (e.g.: remote work, AI) and the role of civil servants in policy implementation. Also, I am interested in exploring emerging paradigms of public sector reform.
